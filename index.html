About the Project

Level 1

Launch of Mission from ISRO's launch PAD, Player can Control the ship with controller

Level 2

Mission to reach in earth's low orbit | Kamran Line 25 Kilo Meters Above from surface

Level 3

Docking Gaganyaan with ISS | player can refule, connect submodules with space craft.

Level 4

Defy the earths gravity by using gravitational slingshot maneuver to enter the Martian gravitational field.

Level 5

Your Gaganyaan with Life Support Module Orbiting Mars, and Scanning for safe spot to land.

Level 6

At this stage Gaganyaan divides into 4 separate modules, Releases a crash test drone to surface

Level 7

Be carefull with the dust particles, steep slopes and crates while landing on the red rocky planet

Level 8

After landing, setup a pressurized habitable hub for living 21 Sols, make sure to connect rover and energy module with hub.

Level 9

witness the magnificiant view of multiple PSLV's arriving on mars with-- seeds, equipments, machinery and machinery with a team.
